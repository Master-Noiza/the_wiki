## Basic Information:
- God of emptiness, order, silence, peace, final rest, infinity, the stillness of death, sleep, untouched nature, natural order.
- It is said that when everything returns to ash, dust, and silence, when the screams fade away, the Silent King will smile.
- **Embodiment of Final Rest:** The Silent King represents the ultimate peace that only death can bring, a state of absolute silence beyond mortal comprehension.
- **Order in Emptiness:** He embodies the natural order of life’s end, the quiet truth that all things must return to dust and silence. His presence is not comforting but inevitable.
- **Detachment and Unyielding Presence:** The Silent King is the embodiment of emotional distance. He does not mourn or celebrate - he simply *is*, an eternal witness to the end of all things, unmoved by mortal concerns.

<br>

## Symbolism:
*The Silent King* is often shown as a towering, shadowy figure draped in a simple, featureless robe that cascades around him like midnight fog. His face is typically obscured by darkness, with only the faintest outline of his solemn features visible, as if he himself is fading into the void.
Artists typically depict him standing on the shores of a silent lake, or on a barren field, with mist rising around him and moonlight barely breaking through, casting long, haunting shadows.
His presence evokes a sense of inevitable quiet, where even whispers are swallowed by silence.
Writers describe his gaze as a void, watching impassively as life fades, unperturbed by the grief or joy of mortals.

**Holy Symbol:**
- **A moonlit lake, its surface perfectly unbroken and still, covered in mist**, a serene image that symbolizes the absolute stillness and finality he governs.

**Holy Colour:**
- **Ashen Grey**, the color of dust, stone, and the remnants of a world long forgotten. This color is neither dark nor light but a neutral tone, reflecting his nature as the ultimate equalizer.

**Holy Number:**
- **Zero**, symbolizing the void, absolute silence, and the absence of all things - an eternal, empty nothingness that awaits all.

**Holy Animal:**
- **The Owl**, a creature that moves silently and sees through darkness. Owls, often perceived as omens of death, embody the watchful stillness of The Silent King. They are creatures of the night, presiding over quiet and shadow, which reflect his domain over the end and the absence of sound.

<br>

## Ideals:

**Embrace Silence.**

- Speak only when necessary, for words are disturbances in the stillness. Seek solitude, reflection, and the quiet of mind.

**All Shall Return to the Still Waters**

- Death is not to be feared but respected. The dead are sacred and should be buried near silent waters if possible, allowing their souls to drift into the King’s realm with ease.

**Balance is Not to Be Broken**

- Undeath, resurrection, and the denial of final rest are abominations that defy the natural order. Seek to end such disruptions and return the dead to the peace of the void.

**Hold Reverence for Stillness**

- Moments of quiet reveal deeper truths than words or action. The Silent King values solitude and solemnity; to disturb the silence is to disturb his peace.
