## Basic Information:
- **Description**: Razorgrass is a tall, blade-like grass species commonly found across the rolling plains of central Vilenought and the Northern Expanse. Growing up to a meter in height,this hardy plant is named for its blade-like leaves, which can cause painful cuts to unprepared travelers and animals alike. Its edges are sharp enough to slice through cloth and lightly armored mounts, presenting a hidden danger in otherwise open landscapes.

- **Usage**: In times of extreme scarcity, Razorgrass can be prepared as a survival food. Thorough cooking can render it marginally edible, though its fibrous texture and bitter taste make it unappealing unless no other food source is available.

<br>

**Hazardous Blades**:

- The edges of Razorgrass are naturally honed and sharp, posing a serious risk to any unprotected who pass through. The blades can slice through light armor and saddle gear, requiring extra caution and preparation from travelers.

**Natural Defence Barriers**:

- Due to its sharp edges, razorgrass acts as a natural barrier, discouraging travel and grazing in areas where it grows densely.

**Survival Resource**:

- While unpleasant in taste, the plant can be used as an emergency food source if thoroughly cooked to break down its fibrous structure. The nutritional value is minimal, but it can sustain life in dire situations.
