# Rainee's Herbs & Poultices

---
Rainee's is a humble, yet reliable apothecary in the village of [[1. The Compendium/B_Atlas/1. The Northern Expanse/Settlements/Respite]]. Just a short walk from the town square it offers local herbs, bandages, poultrices and first aid to those who need it.
