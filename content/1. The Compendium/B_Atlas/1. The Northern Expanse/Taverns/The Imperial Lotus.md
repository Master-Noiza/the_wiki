## Description:
- The Imperial Lotus is the sole Urashalyr establishment in Arkhold, offering a taste of the empire's flavors and culture. Decorated with carved wooden panels, ornate tapestries, and bronze lanterns, it’s a unique sight in the otherwise stark, stone-built city.
- The tavern is managed with care to maintain traditional Urashalyr ambiance, from its soft music to incense that mingles with the spices of foreign dishes. The locals keep their distance, preferring their own inns, but a few curious souls occasionally visit for the mysterious cuisine or a rare cup of coffee.

<br>

## Location:
- Arkhold, on the outskirts of the main market square, near the Urashalyr garrison
- Due to its isolated placement, it is easily avoided by those uninterested in the foreign presence
- The tavern’s exterior resembles the architecture of the local buildings, but inside it is a vibrant, warm environment adorned with tapestries, low tables, and cushions.

## Staff:

**Owner**:

- **K’halan Muralok**: A retired Urashalyr military captain, strict yet kindhearted, values the preservation of Urashalyr culture in Arkhold. He runs the tavern with disciplined warmth, preferring things to stay “as they are at home.”

**Cook/Chef**:

- **Avani Dalin**: A native of the empire’s southern provinces, Dalin is known for her culinary skills and pride in Urashalyr cuisine, delighting customers with her unique spice blends.

**Serving Staff**:

- **Yassir Chahl**: A lively and friendly server, Yassir is the senior server and eager to introduce patrons to the rich flavors of Urashalyr. With a knack for storytelling, he keeps guests entertained between courses.

- **Khalil**: Bartender, quick-witted, brings a sense of humor to the tavern

- **Akosh** and **Salen**: Attentive servers, familiar with the Urashalyr customs and efficient

<br>

## History:

- The tavern, once a simple local drinking hole, was claimed by Urashalyr officials shortly after Arkhold’s forced integration into the empire. Its transformation into an Urashalyr-style establishment was meant as a symbol of cultural exchange but has instead become a point of tension. Though Arkhold’s residents have adapted to the empire’s rule, few are eager to embrace its customs, but it soon grew into an iconic Urashalyr fixture for soldiers and visitors alike.


<br>

## Rumors and Particularities:

- It’s said the Heartland's Warmth has a hidden cellar where imperial soldiers hold clandestine meetings.
- Locals believe the tavern to be haunted by the spirit of the last Monkhalyr innkeeper, who vanished during the conquest.
